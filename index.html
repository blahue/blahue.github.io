Dieter Rams:
10 Principles of Good Deisgn

Good deisgn is...
1. innovative.
2. makes a product useful.
3. aesthic.
4. makes a product understandble.
5. unobtrusive.
6. honest.
7. long-lasting.
8. thorough down to the last detail.
9. environmentally friendly.
10. as little design as possible.
