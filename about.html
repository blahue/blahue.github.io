About Me

I am a college student studying Industrial Design at Southern Illinois University in Carbondale, IL. I grew up in Newburgh, IN near Evansville. Design is my passion and I work hard on every project to make it the best it can be. I firmly believe in Dieter Rams' principles of design and use them as a basis for every design I create.

I firmly believe in Dieter Rams' principles of design and use them as a basis for every design I create. I try to innovate a product that fits the client and also contains part of my personal touch. I’ve completed several products with different audiences such as a lunchbox, iPod dock, lifting mechanism, record player, cupcake mold, and park bench.

In addition to designing products and digital art, I have also created a wide range of art in both two-dimensional and three-dimensional. I’ve worked with various materials including wood, cardboard, wire, clay, plaster, wax, and paint. I strongly feel that having skills in other mediums and doing hands-on work has aided me to improve my ability to design in new and original ways.
